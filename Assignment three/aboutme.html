About Me

I am a UX design student, social psychologist and hot yogi (with a bit of a nerdy side) based in Malmö, Sweden who recently discovered that I have been a designer at heart al along.

I found myself drawn to UX design and was happy to discover that my strenghts of being detail-oriented, quick and structured fell right into place when starting my UX design journey.

Prior to my "design discovery" I managed to collect six years of experience within company development, education and methodical work.

My background allows me to be multifaceted and able to keep a balance between being thoughtful and making fast decisions. I view people’s differences as an advantage and I care about making the world a better place, hopefully while having a bit of fun along the way.
